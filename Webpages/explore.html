Get out and ride

Wherever your day takes you, Dublin Bikes can get you there – and to a coffee stop in between.

  • Phoenix Park Loop
    A local favorite. Scenic, peaceful, and completely car-free — it's one of Europe’s largest city parks and perfect for cruising.
  • Grand Canal Greenway
    Glide past calm waters and leafy paths as you cycle along the Grand Canal. A quiet, refreshing escape right in the city.
  • Samuel Beckett Bridge to Docklands
    Ride riverside along modern glass and steel, through Dublin’s ever-evolving Docklands. Big views. Bigger vibes.
  • Howth Coastal Ride
    Start in the city, end by the sea. This ride rewards you with epic coastal views, cliff paths, and some of the best seafood around.
  • St. Stephen's Green & Grafton Street
    Bike your way through Georgian streets and blooming gardens, then park nearby and explore the heart of Dublin on foot.
  • Royal Canal Way
    Cycle along this peaceful path that traces the canal from the heart of the city to the northwestern suburbs. A quiet, scenic ride dotted with bridges, locks, and unexpected charm.
