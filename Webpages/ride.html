Expanding access to bikes

Dublin Bikes makes it easier than ever to get around the city, with hundreds of bikes and docking stations conveniently located across central Dublin.

Get ready to ride

Dublin Bikes can be unlocked from one station
and returned to any other station in the system.
Take a look below to see how to get started,
meet the bikes, and get a quick overview of riding tips.

How it works

Rent a bike

Use the Dublin Bikes app or your key to unlock and start your ride.

Ride your way

Cruise around town or get to your destination fast.

Return anywhere

Park at any Dublin Bikes station across the city when you're done.

Meet the bikes

Our classic bikes are DIY. Or you can get a little boost with our pedal-assist ebikes. Whatever your preference, Dublin Bikes has the right ride for you.

Classic rides

Our classic bicycles were designed for easy riding in an urban environment. They were built to be comfortable for any type of rider, regardless of height or riding ability.

Our e-dublinbikes

Ride farther with e-dublinbikes. Just insert your personal battery into the bike’s basket slot to activate electric assist—perfect for hills, longer commutes, and smooth city cruising.

Riding tips

A few things to keep in mind before you get going.

Stay informed

Keep an eye on our blog and social media channels for updates, events, and seasonal tips.

Plan ahead

Check station availability before heading out to ensure a smooth ride every time.

Be safe

Always wear a helmet, follow traffic signals, and ride in the bike lane where possible.
